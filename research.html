Research Programme

UAFT is a unified theory. That claim carries a responsibility: to demonstrate that the framework holds across every discipline that touches consciousness. Each paper below extends UAFT into a new domain, building toward a consolidated multi-volume work.

Ontology & Metaphysics Published

The foundational paper establishing consciousness as axiomatic, non-dual differentiation as a new ontological category, and the atemporal logic from which time, gravity, and mass emerge. Introduces conscious bias, the constraint taxonomy, and the smoke-and-lens problem.

General Relativity Coming Soon

Gravitytime as the ontological ground of GR's mathematics. Space as experiential consequence rather than ontological reality. Mass as persistent resonance. The equations need not change — what changes is what the mathematics is understood to describe.

Cosmology Coming Soon

The Big Bang reframed as first differentiation. Dark energy as differentiation, dark matter as coherence. Entropy as redistribution of coherence across scales. The field's cumulative record as cosmological principle.

Thermodynamics Coming Soon

Entropy reframed as redistribution of coherence, not decay. The second law as partial perception of delayed balancing. Energy as the activity of balance, not a substance consumed.

Information Theory Coming Soon

Information is not consciousness. The byte has no valence. Shannon entropy reframed through UAFT's account of constraint and resolution. The distinction between information-for-a-reader and differentiation-for-itself.

Volume 2 — Life: Biology, Evolution, and Neuroscience
Evolutionary Biology Coming Soon

The field as living record driving evolutionary refinement. Why complexity increases. Instinct as field information expressed through biological form. Emotion as evolutionary driver, not survival tool. The emotional tree: three innate roots branching into finer differentiations.

Origin of Life / Abiogenesis Coming Soon

Abiogenesis reframed as the field crossing the first threshold through chemistry. Why life appears. Why it complexifies. The cell as minimal differentiation unit maintaining atemporal connection.

Neuroscience Coming Soon

Deep engagement with Damasio's somatic marker hypothesis. Neural correlates of consciousness reframed as the medium, not the producer. The binding problem dissolved through non-dual differentiation. Microtubules and anesthesia selectivity as evidence for the two-threshold model.

Sleep Science Coming Soon

Sleep as integration — the field's cycle of emergence and return expressed biologically. REM as awareness peeking at the field's consolidation work. Dreams as glimpses of integration. Sleep deprivation as blocked integration overloading the differentiation system.

Sociology & Cultural Theory Coming Soon

Philosophy of Religion & Contemplative Traditions Coming Soon

Buddhist emptiness as the undifferentiated field state. Mystical experience as approach to unity. Not mysticism but structural prediction.
