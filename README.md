🔬 **What is Unified Axioconscious Field Theory (UAFT)?**

UAFT is a foundational framework treating consciousness not as a property that emerges from matter, but as an atemporal ground from which both physical and experiential differentiation arise. The framework integrates work in philosophy of mind, theoretical psychology, quantum biology, and consciousness studies to address questions that conventional approaches have left unresolved.

**Key concepts of UAFT include:**

* **Axioconsciousness** – The atemporal, self-grounding ground of all differentiation
* **Differentiation** – The temporal mechanism through which axioconscious reach produces structure
* **Codifferential** – The non-dual relationship between gravity and time as paired expressions of one process
* **The pinch point** – The structural threshold at which emotional saturation produces an integrated self
* **Binary Severance Threshold (BST)** – Structural account of which substrates can support consciousness
* **Conscious Bias Taxonomy** – Twelve recurring patterns of inherited assumption that produce framework-bound paradoxes
